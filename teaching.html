Teaching

Teaching is relational, not transactional. I design environments where students practise, reflect, ask questions, and recover from mistakes without shame.

Approach

  • Mastery practice with frequent, low‑stakes checks
  • Fast, actionable feedback cycles
  • Transparent criteria and humane grading
  • Supportive conversations guided by data, not defined by it

Student voices

“I stopped being afraid of mistakes.”
“The mastery quizzes helped me breathe and try again.”
“Now I know how to start — and how to recover when I wobble.”

Supervision

Structured milestones, supportive challenge, and ethical research practice.
